MAP
OF
AFRICA

Map of Africa, the world's second-largest and
second-most-populous continent is located on the
African continental plate. The "Dark Continent" is
divided roughly into two parts by the equator and
surrounded by sea except where the Isthmus of Suez
joins it to Asia. In the north, the continent is bounded
by the Mediterranean Sea and the Strait of Gibraltar, in
the west by the Atlantic Ocean, in the south and the
southeast by the Indian Ocean, and on its north-
eastern coast by the Red Sea, and the Gulf of Aden.
